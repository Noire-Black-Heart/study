GOMS: goals: describe user's ultimate goals
, operators:  elementary use	r action (press button), measure in time duration
, methods:  learned procedure for a goal(sequence of actions) 
, selection rules: decides which method to select when there is more than 1

eg google search
goal: to search stuff
operator: move mouse, press keyboard, press mouse button
method: open browser, move mouse to input, input search key, press enter, profit
selection rule: the method that costs fewer time
